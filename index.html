Power Level Game

You will be given a random target power level at the start of the game.

There are four fighters below. By clicking on a fighter you will add a specific amount of points to your total power level.

You win the game by matching your total power level to the random number. You lose the game if your total power level goes above the random number.

The value of each fighter is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each fighter.
